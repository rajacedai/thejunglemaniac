About
me

Abinash Poudel named and founded the Jungle Maniac community on August 23rd, 2021. The major goal of the Jungle Maniac was to develop a community of travel enthusiasts and to provide companionship to others who enjoy traveling and trekking but are alone and in need of company. The Jungle Maniac group currently boasts six team members who are also travel aficionados, and the community has provided companionship to many people. More than 50 individuals, including some foreigners, have previously participated in the Jungle Maniac's various events such as short hiking, long hiking, downhill cycling, water fall searching, deep jungle exploring, and so on.


We are primarily based in Pokhara. There are numerous unexplored areas surrounding Pokhara City. Join us as we delve into the unknown. When we go on an adventure activity such as a short hike, cycling, camping, trekking, or an off-road bike ride, we will post about it on our social media page. If you are nearby, you can call or message us and easily be ready to explore with us... Thank you!!
